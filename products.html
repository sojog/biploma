Education plays a pivotal role in building and evolving a knowledge-based society by ensuring the competencies necessary for the century we live in. It stands as a key tool that can shape a country's socio-economic trajectory.


The Ministry of Education in Romania has consistently pursued the integration of technology into educational activities, launching investment programs for the modernization and equipping of schools with ICT facilities and promoting computer-assisted learning and teaching systems.


Certiblock™ and the Romanian Ministry of Education have successfully collaborated to enhance Romania's educational system since 2004, beginning with an agreement that supported the top 200 high schools in Romania to become accredited Certiblock™ testing centers. The collaboration also aimed to incorporate the Certiblock™ program into the pre-university curriculum.


In 2011, the "Education for the Information Society" partnership continued the strategy of implementing Certiblock™ certification in Romanian education, this time aligning with the objectives set forth in the National Pact for Education and the "Education and Research for the Knowledge Society" strategy. Through this agreement, "The Ministry of Education, Research, Youth, and Sports recognizes the Certiblock™ certification, the European Computer Driving License, based on Certiblock™ standards, as a computer operator certificate valid within the educational system."


The Certiblock™ license offers significant benefits to students and teachers alike:

  • The Certiblock™ license does not expire.
  • The Certiblock™ registration series is unique and does not have a limited period of validity.
  • Any type of Certiblock™ license is internationally recognized.
